The 176 Layer AI Cake

A layered digital transformation that is swapping manual training sessions for AI at scale, one layer at a time.

⏱ Ongoing 🏢 7 business units 📋 176 active programs 🔧 Synthesia · Articulate · CDB · Excel

The kind of cake that only gets better with time. AI powered, process driven, and still rising.

Ingredients

  • AI-powered training via Synthesia
  • Interactive e-learning via Articulate
  • CDB & ReqMan process tools
  • Master data across 176 programs
  • Automated project assessments
  • A mandate to go fully digital

Method

1
Start with the PMO as it is. Manual sessions, scattered data, training that does not scale. Understanding the mess is the first ingredient.
2
Replace every in-person session with AI-powered videos via Synthesia and e-learning via Articulate. Do not rush this layer. It holds everything else together.
3
Fold in CDB and ReqMan. Document processes, manage requirements, replace anything still running on memory. Test monthly. Adjust seasoning as needed.
4
Pour in the data. Cleanse across 176 programmes and 7 units. Bake until the dashboards update in real time. Garnish with audit ready documentation and serve hot.
Chef's note: This cake pairs exceptionally well with cross-functional teams, and a bank ready to think bold. Recipe currently in active development.
